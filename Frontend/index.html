Features

Everything you need to run a smooth operation

From intelligent assignment to real-time notifications — Schedule handles the complexity so you can focus on your people.

Smart Assignment

Automatically match employees to shifts based on availability, skills, and role requirements — cutting scheduling time by up to 80%.

Conflict Detection

Instantly flag double bookings, overtime violations, and unavailability conflicts before they become problems on the floor.

Real-time Notifications

Employees receive instant push and email alerts for new assignments, shift changes, and approvals — no more missed messages.

Advanced Analytics

Track labor costs, shift coverage rates, and employee performance metrics through clear, actionable dashboard reports.

Role-based Access

Granular permissions for Admins, Managers, and Employees — everyone sees exactly what they need, nothing more.

Mobile-first Design

Fully responsive across all devices. Managers can update schedules and employees can check shifts from anywhere, any time.
